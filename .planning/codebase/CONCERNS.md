# Concerns & Risks

- **Database Volatility**: Frequent resets during development wipe all reputation history; requires robust seeding scripts.
- **Sync Latency**: Time between submission approval and on-chain SBT issuance can lead to UI "stale" states.
- **Mobile Responsiveness**: High-fidelity 3D and complex bento grids require careful optimization for smaller viewports.
- **Identity Sybil**: Reputation system needs stronger verification to prevent multi-account XP farming.
